I'm passionate about applying machine learning to solve meaningful real-world problems.
I studied Computer Science at [Technical University Berlin](https://www.tu-berlin.de), with a focus on the theory and applications of machine intelligence.
In my [Master's thesis](/masters-thesis) at [Fraunhofer HHI](https://www.hhi.fraunhofer.de/), I explored how to train neural networks more efficiently using gradient compression during backpropagation.
Currently, I work on embedded speech enhancement and other audio processing tasks for our market-leading hearing aids at [Audatic](https://audatic.ai).
